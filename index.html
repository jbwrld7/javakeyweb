Privacy Policy

The JavaKey (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data of students who use our website and game progress tracking system. By accessing or using this platform, you agree to the terms described in this policy.


We collect certain types of information to provide a better experience for users. This includes personal details such as your full name, email address, section or class, and encrypted account credentials. We also gather gameplay data such as scores, completion times, achievements, and progress levels, along with technical information like your device type, browser version, and IP address. These details help us monitor your activity, track your progress, and improve overall performance within the platform.


The information we collect is primarily used to track student progress, display achievements and leaderboards, provide personalized feedback, and ensure secure access to the system. We also analyze user data to enhance the gameplay experience and maintain platform reliability. We want to emphasize that we do not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or unauthorized third parties. Data is only accessible to authorized administrators, instructors, or trusted service providers, such as Firebase, who are bound by confidentiality agreements.


To protect your data, we use secure storage systems and encryption methods to prevent unauthorized access, modification, or loss. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will immediately notify affected users and take the necessary steps to resolve the issue. Users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal data. For any privacy-related concerns, users may contact us at javakey.team@gmail.com


Our website may use cookies to maintain login sessions and store user preferences for a smoother experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may limit certain features of the website. The JavaKey Game Progress Tracker is designed primarily for students under educational supervision, and we comply with children’s privacy regulations by ensuring that no personal information is knowingly collected from children under 13 without proper consent.


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the revised version will include a new effective date. Continued use of the website after such updates will constitute acceptance of the new terms.


For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at javakey.team@gmail.com

About JavaKey

JavaKey is an interactive and cross-platform learning application designed to make learning Java programming more engaging, visual, and game-like. The platform provides gamified lessons, challenges, and quizzes that allow learners to practice and apply Java concepts in real-time.


Users can track their learning progress through a web-based progress tracker that syncs across devices. Whether you're a beginner learning Java syntax or an advanced coder mastering object-oriented programming, JavaKey helps you stay motivated and have fun while improving your coding skills.


Our mission is to make coding education accessible, exciting, and effective for all. Learn Java the fun way — with JavaKey.
